Breakout DELUXE

About the game

A re-creation of the game "Breakout" using HTML, CSS, and JS


How to play

Press the "start" button to begin the game. Use the paddle to bounce the ball and break the bricks at the top of the screen. Be careful, don't let the ball fall off the screen or else you'll lose a life. Break all of the bricks to win the game. Lose all of your lives and its GAME OVER.


Controls

Keyboard: Use the left and right arrow keys to move the paddle
Mouse: Move your mouse from side to side to move the paddle Touchscreen: Slide your finger left and right to move the paddle
